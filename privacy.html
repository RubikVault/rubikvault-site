Privacy Policy

This privacy notice explains, in a compact way, which personal data may be processed when you visit rubikvault.com.

1. Controller

RubikVault Project
Contact: contact@rubikvault.com

2. Basic principles

The site is operated as a lean, content-focused project. No user accounts, no login areas and no portfolio tracking features are offered. Personal data is only processed to the extent technically necessary for providing and protecting the website.

3. Server log files

When you access this website, the web server may automatically store information such as:

  • IP address (in shortened or pseudonymised form where possible)
  • Date and time of access
  • Visited pages / requested files
  • Browser type and version, operating system
  • Referring URL (website from which the request originates)

This information is used solely for ensuring the technical operation, stability and security of the site. Log files are automatically deleted after a limited retention period unless they are needed to clarify security incidents.

4. Cookies and tracking

The aim is to operate the site with as few cookies and external trackers as possible. Currently, no first-party tracking cookies are intentionally set.

However, some embedded third-party widgets (especially TradingView and potentially video or social media embeds) may place their own cookies or use similar technologies. These are under the sole responsibility of the respective provider. Please refer to the providers’ privacy policies for details.

5. Embedded third-party content

This website integrates third-party content such as:

  • TradingView market widgets (charts, tickers, economic calendar)
  • Links to or embedded content from YouTube, X (Twitter), TikTok and Instagram

When you view such content, the respective providers may process personal data (e.g. IP address, device information, interaction data) under their own responsibility and according to their own terms and privacy policies.

6. Contact

If you contact the project by e-mail, the information you provide will be processed solely for handling the request and any necessary follow-up communication. E-mails are stored only as long as needed for documentation and legal obligations.

7. Your rights

Depending on the applicable data protection law (e.g. GDPR in the European Union), you may have rights such as:

  • Right of access to the personal data processed about you
  • Right to rectification or deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to object to certain processing activities
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, please contact contact@rubikvault.com.

8. Updates to this notice

This privacy notice may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements or the functionality of the website. The date of the most recent update will be indicated here.
