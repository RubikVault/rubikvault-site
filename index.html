Volatility & fear

Volatility indices like the VIX help frame how nervous equity markets are. Spikes often coincide with de-risking and forced selling.

Check the cross-asset tape for confirmation – extreme fear or complacency rarely lasts.

Crypto risk appetite

Crypto tends to behave like high-beta tech. Strong moves in majors vs. equities often signal changing risk appetite rather than isolated “crypto-only“ stories.

Focus on majors first – altcoins are leverage on that behaviour, not a separate asset class.

How to use this dashboard

Three quick steps to make the page part of your daily routine.

  1. Start with the cross-asset tape and mini chart. Is the day broadly risk-on or risk-off? Are equities and crypto moving together or decoupling?
  2. Check today’s macro events. Look for central bank meetings, inflation prints and labour data – they change the regime, not just the intraday noise.
  3. Read the Daily Market Decode. Use the narrative to connect prices, macro and positioning – then decide which parts are actually relevant to you.

About RubikVault

Independent research-style project. No client capital, no asset management, no hidden sponsors.

What it is

RubikVault focuses on cross-asset thinking: how equities, crypto, rates, FX and commodities interact over time. It tries to connect regimes, liquidity and positioning instead of just highlighting the loudest move of the day.

The goal is simple: help you understand why markets behave the way they do – not tell you what to buy or sell.

What it is not

No brokerage, no asset management, no personalised advice, no “signals”, and no paid group. All content is educational and should be treated as one input among many.

Always do your own research and make sure any decision fits your own risk tolerance, time horizon and constraints.
