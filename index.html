Key concepts explained

Global liquidity

In plain terms: how much central-bank money sits in the system. Rising balance sheets are usually a tailwind for risk assets; falling ones are a headwind – especially for expensive growth and crypto.

Risk appetite

When high-yield, tech and crypto all rally together, you are in pure risk-on. When flows rotate into cash, bonds and defensives, the regime is getting tougher.

Liquidity vs rates

High policy rates drain liquidity; low rates push it in. The level matters, but the direction of change and the interaction with fiscal flows matter even more.

How to use this dashboard

  1. Check Market Pulse. Look at indices, sectors and VIX first. That gives you the regime in seconds.
  2. Scan Macro & Liquidity. Combine spending, interest injections and money growth to see whether price moves are liquidity-backed or just sentiment.
  3. Read narratives, not noise. Use sentiment and earnings snippets to understand how companies and investors describe the cycle – then map that back to your own risk exposure.
